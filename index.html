May 11 and 18 @ The RHHS Library

Cheerio

A programming contest run by the RHHS Computer Science Club

About

Cheerio is a series of competitive programming contests run by the RHHS CS Club.


The next contest will be split over two days: Thursday, May 11 and Thursday, May 18 from 4:00 pm to 6:00 pm in the library.


Here are the registration forms. You may participate individually or in teams of up to four.


The contest will be done online using DMOJ, additional contest information can be found on the contest page. Expect some non-traditional programming problems, such as golfing or reverse engineering.


There are $800 worth of prizes as well as framed certificates for the top 5 teams. Expect free food, drinks and stickers!


Hope to see you there!
